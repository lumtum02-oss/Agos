AGOS — DESIGN


ELEVATOR PITCH

Agos (Tagalog: "flow") streams salaries and grants by the second on Stellar.
The payer funds a stream once; the recipient earns continuously and withdraws
the vested amount instantly. XLM streams are escrowed in the AgosStream Soroban
contract, which computes vesting on-chain from ledger time — no batch payday, no
server timer, no trust in a backend ledger.


PROBLEM

1. Money is earned continuously but paid in monthly batches, straining the people
   who can least absorb the delay.
2. Existing early-wage-access tools are custodial and opaque.
3. Grants and stipends are committed up front but released on rigid schedules with
   little transparency for the beneficiary.


SOLUTION

1. The payer opens a stream: recipient address, per-second rate, funded total,
   and asset.
2. XLM streams are funded into the AgosStream Soroban contract with a start/end
   schedule. Vesting is computed on-chain: vested = total * (now - start) /
   (end - start), clamped between zero and the total.
3. The recipient withdraws the vested-but-unwithdrawn amount at any time; the
   contract pays out on-chain to the recipient's fixed address.
4. The payer can stop a stream: the contract settles the vested portion to the
   recipient and reclaims the unvested remainder to the payer.
5. USDC is opt-in via one-tap changeTrust and settles through classic Horizon
   payments.


DEMO / WOW MOMENT

The per-second drip counter on the stream page, mirroring the contract's on-chain
schedule, ticks up in real time. Hit Withdraw and a real Stellar transaction
fires from the contract — the explorer link proves the vested XLM moved on-chain.
Money that is earned every second can now be withdrawn any second.


VISUAL DIRECTION

1. Primary color cyan-600 (#0891b2) — flowing water, the meaning of "agos".
2. Typography: Inter for body, Playfair Display for headings — professional but
   warm.
3. Iconography: Waves (lucide-react) as the core motif.
4. Layout: hero with a live drip counter; dashboard of stat cards + stream grid;
   stream detail with a full drip counter, the on-chain contract panel, and the
   withdraw / stop actions.
