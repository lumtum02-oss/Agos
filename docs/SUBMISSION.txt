AGOS — STELLAR APAC HACKATHON SUBMISSION


PROJECT NAME

Agos (Tagalog: "flow") — payday is every second.


PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Agos turns salaries and grants into a live, on-chain money stream on Stellar.
An employer or grant maker opens a stream for a recipient with a per-second
rate and a funded total. For XLM streams, the full grant is escrowed in the
AgosStream Soroban smart contract with a start/end schedule; the amount that has
vested is computed on-chain from ledger time. The recipient withdraws the vested
amount whenever they want and a real Stellar transaction settles it. There is no
payday and no batch — money is always flowing.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

1. Remote and cross-border teams are paid in monthly batches. Money is earned
   continuously but released in lumps, creating cash-flow stress for workers.
2. Early-wage-access and payroll tools are custodial, opaque, and regional.
3. Grant and stipend programs face the same gap: funds are committed up front but
   released on rigid schedules, with little transparency for the beneficiary.


PROPOSED SOLUTION

1. Replace batch payroll with a streaming primitive. The payer funds a stream
   once; the recipient earns by the second.
2. Escrow XLM streams in the AgosStream Soroban contract. Vesting is derived from
   ledger time on-chain, so the available amount is trustlessly verifiable and
   needs no server timer.
3. Pull-based withdrawal: the recipient withdraws the vested amount at any time
   and the contract pays it out on-chain. The payer can stop a stream early — the
   contract settles the vested portion to the recipient and reclaims the rest.
4. XLM is the default asset (no trustline). USDC is opt-in via a one-tap
   changeTrust, settling through classic Horizon payments.


TARGET USERS / AUDIENCE

1. Startup founders and agencies paying remote contractors across APAC.
2. Freelancers and contractors who want access to wages as they are earned.
3. Grant and stipend programs that want transparent, time-vested disbursement.


SELECTED FOCUS TRACK

Savings & DeFi — programmable, on-chain salary/grant streaming on Stellar.
